Our Story

Creating Memorable Wedding Feasts Since 1995

At Gupta Caterer, Manoj Gupta As a Event Manager we believe that exceptional food is an essential part of any wedding celebration. Our journey began with a simple passion for creating culinary masterpieces that delight the senses and bring people together.

With over a decade of experience catering for weddings across the country, our team of talented chefs and event specialists work tirelessly to ensure that every detail of your wedding catering is absolutely perfect.

We pride ourselves on using only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients to create dishes that are not only delicious but also visually stunning. From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, we tailor our services to match your unique vision and requirements.
